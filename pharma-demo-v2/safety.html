Read this information carefully before taking MediRelief Pro.

Keep this information for future reference. If you have questions, ask your healthcare provider or pharmacist.

üö´ Contraindications

Do NOT take MediRelief Pro if you have any of the following:

  • Known hypersensitivity to Medinophen Complex or any component
  • Severe hepatic impairment (Child-Pugh Class C)
  • Active gastrointestinal bleeding
  • Use within 14 days of MAO inhibitors
  • Pregnancy (third trimester) - Category D
  • Children under 18 years of age

üíä Drug Interactions

Tell your healthcare provider about all medications you are taking:

  • Warfarin: May increase anticoagulant effect - monitor INR
  • SSRIs/SNRIs: Increased risk of serotonin syndrome
  • Alcohol: Enhanced CNS depression - avoid concurrent use
  • CYP3A4 inhibitors: May increase MediRelief Pro levels
  • Other pain medications: Consult healthcare provider before combining
  • Blood pressure medications: May reduce effectiveness

‚ö° Warnings and Precautions

CNS Effects

May cause drowsiness. Do not drive or operate machinery until you know how this medication affects you.

Hepatotoxicity

Risk of liver damage. Do not exceed recommended dose. Avoid alcohol.

Renal Impairment

Dose adjustment may be required for patients with kidney problems.

Elderly Patients

Start with lower doses. Increased sensitivity to side effects.
